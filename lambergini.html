LAMBERGINI

Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A. (Italian: [lamborˈɡiːni]) is an Italian brand and manufacturer of luxury sports cars and SUVs based in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy. The company is owned by the Volkswagen Group through its subsidiary Audi.

MAX HORSE POWER

Motori Marini Lamborghini produces a large V12 marine engine block for use in World Offshore Series Class 1 powerboats. A Lamborghini branded marine engine displaces approximately 8,171 cc (499 cu in) and outputs approximately 940 hp (700 kW).

OVERVIEW

Manufacturer Company is Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A. Production is active from 2014 and present.Assembly is done in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy Designer is Filippo Perini.
